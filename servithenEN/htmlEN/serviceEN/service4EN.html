Installation audits and expertise.

Hot and cold audit

Hot: we inspect your equipment while it is running and stopped to ensure that it is working properly. We take a close look at the equipment to ensure the proper functioning of your installation/equipment Thanks to to our long experience in audits, we recommend improvements or modifications if necessary.

Cold audit: Thanks to our knowledge, we assess your equipment and installations using measurement tools such as (thickness measurement). A deliverable will be provided at the end of the expertise in order to improve or correct to ensure the proper functioning of your equipment. We thus inspect all types of equipment from the inside and outside, example of equipment: oven (Summit, Vault, Burners), recuperator, bag filter, DeNOx, exchanger, bag filter (Analysis in a certified laboratory), catalyst (Analysis in a certified laboratory)
The cold audit is a more in-depth inspection where we target the faulty or damaged equipment that we identify during operation of the installation.
