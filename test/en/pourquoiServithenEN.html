Presentation

The company Servithen (Services Thermique Environnement) was created in 1986. Mainly centered at the origin on the audit and the bringing into conformity of existing thermal installations, it extended its services to the installation of lines incineration and treatment of fumes or gaseous effluents and the supply of specific equipment, or "turnkey" installations, with in particular:

· Furnaces,

· Flue gas scrubbers for dust reduction and acid gas neutralization,

· Wet electrostatic filters allowing high dust collection efficiencies,

Units dry treatment of incineration fumes,

· Installations for the treatment of volatile organic compounds (VOC), odors or deNOx treatment.

The SERVITHEN Company was awarded the Gazelle 2005 label by the Minister of SMEs, Trade, Crafts and Liberal Professions, Mr. Renaud DUTREIL. This trophy rewards 2,000 French companies out of 1.2 million SME. The gazelles selected are the SMEs which grew during the period studied from 1993 to 2003, i.e. two or three times faster than those in the same sector of activity, i.e. those which are in the top 5 or 10% of their population in terms of of growth and which have a higher profitability than that of their counterparts. The objective of this government program is to help maintain the rapid growth of these most dynamic SMEs. In particular, by mobilizing all the skills to enable them to support their growth, in particular by enriching their relational network, and by giving them access to specific offers from partners, among other financiers
